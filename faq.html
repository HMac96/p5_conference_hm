FAQ

When Will Registration Open?

Registration for the 2020 conference will open on March 15, 2020. Registration ends May 09, 2020.

Can I volunteer at the Conference?

DCI members may volunteer for the conference, and are regularly recruited to volunteer during the conference. To learn more about volunteering email: volunteer@dci.org.

When & Where is DCI Conference held?

DCI Conference is held in Washington D.C. every year in May. The Conference will be held at Arena Stage in the The Mead Center at 1101 Sixth Street SW Washington, DC 20024.

What is DCI Conference?

Each year DCI Conference brings the best speakers and creatives to D.C., to present on the latest improvements in the web, design, and, interactive fields. Events range from breakout sessions to hands-on workshops for attendees to sharpen their skills and build their business in the digital age.
